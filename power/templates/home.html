Who we are

Current Automation, located in Kya Sand, Randburg, began operations in 1994 and has accumulated a lot of knowledge in supplying POWER SOLUTIONS to the southern African market over the last 16 years. We decided to expand into all Power Related product lines and is continuously expanding into all power and alternative power energy products to obtain and sustain a dominating part of the power market demand, by supplying quality as well as cost effective solutions.
